Frequently Asked Questions
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel anytime. No long-term commitment. Your data stays safe even if you cancel — you just move back to the Free plan.
What happens to my data if I don't upgrade?
Your data stays safely stored on your device with the Free plan. You won't lose anything. Upgrading adds cloud backup so your data is also secured online.
Is ₹99/month for one device or all devices?
Pro plan covers your shop across all devices. Login from phone, tablet, or desktop — your data stays synced everywhere.
How do I pay? Is UPI accepted?
Yes! We accept UPI, debit card, credit card, and net banking. Payment is simple and done in under 1 minute.
क्या Hindi में support मिलेगी? (Is Hindi support available?)
