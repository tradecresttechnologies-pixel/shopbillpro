📋 Compliance Reports — Pro feature

Generate Hotel Register (Form B) and FRRO Form C for foreign guests. Required by Indian hotel regulations. Upgrade to unlock.

📊
Performance KPIs — the three numbers that matter for hotel revenue management: Occupancy % (room-nights sold ÷ available), ADR (avg daily rate — room revenue per night sold), and RevPAR (revenue per available room — occupancy × ADR). Excludes cancellations and no-shows. Room revenue is GST-exclusive.
📅
Daily Operations — arrivals expected to check in, departures expected to check out, and guests currently in-house. Use this for daily handover at shift change, or printed at the front desk for housekeeping coordination.
📋
Daily Manager Report — single-page executive summary for end-of-day handover or morning meeting. Combines KPIs, occupancy, arrivals/departures, and revenue summary for one specific date. Print on A4 portrait.
📒
Hotel Register (Form B) — required under Indian Police Act / state Hotel Rules. Captures every arrival in the date range. Print on A4 landscape; bind into the official register. Fields not stored digitally (father's/husband's name, occupation, last/next destination) are left blank for handwritten entry at the desk.
🛂
FRRO Form C — Bureau of Immigration requires every hotel to register foreign guests within 24 hours of arrival via indianfrro.gov.in. This view shows foreign guests only. Use CSV for portal upload, Print for the in-house copy. Fields not collected digitally (sex, DOB, passport place/date of issue, port of arrival) remain blank.
